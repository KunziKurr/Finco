Meet our team

Martin M. Muthengi

Director and Board Chairman

Mr. Muthengi has been practicing law for the last 24 years and is the Managing Partner at Muttisya & Co. Advocates. He is a Commissioner for Oaths, Notaries Public, company secretaries and Arbitrator. Mr. Muthengi has a LLB (Hons) UoN, Diploma in Law (KSL) and is a CPS (K). He has vast experience in handling criminal cases, civil litigation, conveyance work, arbitration and company secretarial work.

Paul M. Muteta

irector

Mr. Muteta is an architect and has been practicing over the last three decades. He is passionate about empowerment of youth and women for economic development. He has overseen numerous projects across the country and is experienced in project design and management. He chairs the strategy committee in the company.

Susan Mumbi Ng’ang’a

Micro-Finance Expert

Susan is an experienced microfinance expert with over 8 years in the industry. She has extensive knowledge in credit management, credit operations and resource management. She has previously worked for credit only microfinance institutions, managing a branch in Nakuru. She is responsible for daily operations at Karibu, guiding the implementations of our strategy.
