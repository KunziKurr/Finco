Our Risk Management Matrix:-

Most risks for lenders include credit, operational, market, and liquidity risk. We have a well-constructed risk management infrastructure in line with existing policies and regulations.

Credit Risk

Credit risk-It occurs when borrowers or counterparties fail to meet contractual obligations. To lower the risk exposure, we lend money to people with good credit histories, transact with high-quality counterparties, or request for collateral to back up the loans.

Operational Risk

Operational risk is the risk of loss due to errors, interruptions, or damages caused by people, systems, or processes. The operational type of risk is low for our business operations considering our lending is secured and follow clear collateral transfer process. This will be followed strictly.

Market Risk

Market risk-It is due to the unpredictability of equity markets, commodity prices, interest rates, and credit spreads. Since changes in commodity prices are caused by supply and demand shifts that are often hard to predict, we will decrease market risk by diversification of investments.

Liquidity Risk

Liquidity risk refers to the ability of a company to access cash to meet funding obligations. We mitigate this by maintaining a healthy 80% lending and 20% in Cash and Cash equivalents.
