Our Growth Strategy

To our stakeholders, these pillars are significant to our growth strategy:-

People

Our staff are young, experienced and energetic. We have invested into training and building capacity to ensure the microfinance provides outstanding customer experience.

Entrepreneurship Development

We strive to support businesses especially among youth and women to create their own employment through entrepreneurship development. Further, our products respond to the specific business needs and processes.

Technology

Kenya is a leading hub for technology innovations and adoption. We use the opportunities provided by technology to serve our customers.

Quality portfolio growth

Enabled by experienced and friendly staff, we have built a quality portfolio that guarantees returns to all stakeholders.

Areas of business

Small and medium enterprises (SMEs)

In trade, Karibu is targeting micro, small and medium enterprises that operate retail shops, wholesale, manufacture, transport, crop vendors, boda boda operators, food vendors, hoteliers, tailors, carpenters and so on to expand access to credit.

Agriculture

In agribusiness, Karibu seeks to not only provide better agricultural finance services but build capacity of the agribusiness small and medium enterprises to access financing. This will expand trade in key commodities including livestock and crops.
